Indiana Jones

Raiders of the Lost Ark

Ford portrays Indiana Jones, a globe-trotting archaeologist vying with Nazi German forces in 1936 to recover the long-lost Ark of the Covenant, a relic said to make an army invincible.

Temple of Doom

After arriving in India, Indiana Jones is asked by desperate villagers to find a mystical stone and rescue their children from a Thuggee cult practicing child slavery, black magic, and ritualistic human sacrifice in honor of the goddess, Kali.

The Last Crusade

Indiana searches for his father, a Holy Grail scholar, who has been captured by Nazis while on a journey to find the Holy Grail.
